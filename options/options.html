Todorima – Optimal Reminder — Settings

Customize notifications, snooze, and catch-up behavior after sleep.

Notifications

Keeps notifications visible until you dismiss them (may be ignored by OS).
If off, Snooze buttons are hidden in notifications and lists.

Snooze

Display

If off, hides tag fields and tag search.
If on, the Completed section is collapsed initially.

Language

Catch-up After Sleep

On resume, notify tasks that became due within this window while away.

Data Management

Set 0 to disable. Default is 30 days.
Settings are saved in chrome.storage.sync and synced across signed-in browsers.
